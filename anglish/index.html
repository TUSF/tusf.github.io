This little tool it meant to highlight words in English derived from non-Germanic tungs.

The compiled list of terms comes from Wiktionary's category listings for "English terms derived from X", where X is some non-Germanic language.

At the moment, only words categorized as English terms derived from Anglo-Norman or from Old French are highlighted. Plus, because of lazy regex, it's likely to not capture most conjugations of words it should have, unless Wiktionary had an Entry for that form, and categorized the page properly. French/Norman Affixes are also not captured.

key:
text — Anglo-Norman
text — Old French

It's not perfect, so maybe you can edit the anglonorman.txt and oldfrench.txt files?
